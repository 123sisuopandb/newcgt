Solana Brain Wallets

Solana uses Ed25519 cryptography. A brain wallet passphrase is hashed to create a 32-byte seed, from which the Ed25519 keypair is derived.

Solana's high throughput means sweeper bots can act within milliseconds. Any funds sent to a weak brain wallet address are stolen almost instantly.
