What is a Vanity Address?

A vanity address is a cryptocurrency address that contains a custom pattern you choose, such as your name, brand, or a memorable word. For example, a Bitcoin vanity address might look like 1CAFE... or 1YourName.... The address is cryptographically valid and functions identically to any randomly generated address.

How Does Vanity Address Generation Work?

Vanity addresses are created through brute-force search. The generator creates random private keys, derives their addresses, and checks if the address matches your desired pattern. This process repeats until a match is found. The more characters you want to match, the longer it takes - each additional character increases difficulty by approximately 58x for Bitcoin or 16x for EVM addresses.

Generation Speed and Difficulty

Modern browsers using WebAssembly can generate thousands of addresses per second. For Bitcoin, expect ~4,000-6,000 addresses/second per thread. For EVM chains, ~8,000 addresses/second per thread. A 4-character prefix typically takes seconds, while 6+ characters may take hours or days depending on your hardware.

Is It Secure?

Yes, when generated properly. This tool runs entirely in your browser - no private keys are ever sent to any server. The underlying cryptography is identical to standard addresses. However, always verify you're using the official tool and never share your private key with anyone.

Use Cases for Vanity Addresses

Vanity addresses are popular for businesses wanting branded donation addresses, individuals creating memorable personal addresses, or for easier visual verification of frequently-used addresses. They add a personal touch while maintaining full cryptographic security.
