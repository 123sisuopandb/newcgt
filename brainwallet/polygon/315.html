Polygon Brain Wallets

Polygon brain wallets work identically to Ethereum - the same passphrase generates the same address. This makes cross-chain sweeping trivial for attackers.

Lower gas fees on Polygon make it easier for bots to sweep small amounts that wouldn't be profitable on Ethereum mainnet.
